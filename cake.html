About

7th Heaven began its journey in 2004 as a small cafe in Out of the Blue, Pali Hill, Bandra. Today, 7th Heaven is one of the most reputed patisserie brands in Mumbai. Bunty Mahajan, who trained at Le Cordon Bleu, has an enduring passion for baking, which is evidenced by the innovative products 7th Heaven offers. Product quality and the consumer are the pillars of the 7th Heaven brand. Whether a small gathering or a grand celebration, 7th Heaven looks to make the occasion memorable and special, with its creative, mouth-watering cakes 7th Heaven specializes in customized wedding cakes, anniversary cakes, engagement cakes, childrens' birthday cakes, designer cakes, baby shower cakes, theme cakes, photo cakes, celebration cakes, bachelorette party cakes, customized cupcakes, dessert catering and gifting with wedding invites, Diwali gifting, corporate gifting and birth announcement gifting. The newly launched health dessert line focuses on Keto, Vegan, Gluten free & Sugar free desserts. We take custom cake orders on Whatsapp & phone and non-custom cake orders on our online ordering platform. We accept online payments, and deliver your cake for you, so you have an effortless ordering experience, from your home.
